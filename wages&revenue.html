Wages & Revenue Analysis

Domain : Finance & Accounting

Function :

Performed global wages and revenue analysis for three regions - Asia, Europe, and North America.

Project Overview:

Developed a Power BI dashboard to perform analysis, producing quantitative visualizations in Power BI to draw valuable insights based on different parameters affecting the wages and revenue of the three regions.

Problem Statements:

The company wants to know the wages and revenue performance of the three regions and accordingly provide business solutions.

Methodology:

Datasets were downloaded from Kaggle, then normalized and cleaned in Power BI. Developed a Power BI dashboard to perform analysis, producing quantitative visualizations in Power BI to draw valuable insights based on different parameters affecting the wages and revenue of the three regions.

Success Criteria:

Successfully performed global wages and revenue analysis for three regions - Asia, Europe, and North America. Developed a Power BI dashboard to perform analysis, producing quantitative visualizations in Power BI to draw valuable insights based on different parameters affecting the wages and revenue of the three regions. Provided business solutions to the company based on the insights derived from the analysis.
